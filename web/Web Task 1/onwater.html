Fun on the Water

One of the main attractions of The Mount is it's beautiful coastline and easy access to the clear, warm waters of the Pacific Ocean.

The town offers a huge range of on-the-water activities, including water-skiing, tube-riders, kite-surfing, wake-boarding, kayaking and fishing.
